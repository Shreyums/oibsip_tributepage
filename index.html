Tribute to Puneet Rajkumar

About Puneet Rajkumar

Puneet Rajkumar was a renowned Indian actor in the Kannada film industry. He was born on March 17, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He hailed from the prestigious Rajkumar family, known for their contributions to Kannada cinema.

Puneet Rajkumar made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Premada Kanike" in 1976. He later appeared in several successful films, earning him a huge fan following. Some of his notable movies include "Appu," "Milana," "Rajakumara," "Natasarvabhouma,"and "Yuvarathna."

Known for his charismatic on-screen presence and versatile acting skills, Puneet Rajkumar received numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. He was not only an actor but also a playback singer and a television presenter.

Legacy

Puneet Rajkumar's sudden demise on October 29, 2021, left his fans and the film industry in shock. His contributions to Kannada cinema and his philanthropic endeavors will always be remembered. Puneet Rajkumar's legacy lives on through his remarkable performances and the love of his fans.
